## 1. Branded vs Non-Branded

**Purpose:** Measure brand awareness, isolate "pure" organic SEO traffic.

| Filter | Regex (Matches regex) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Branded | `(?i)(brandname\|brand\-name\|brandvariant)` | Adapt variants to the client's brand |
| Non-branded | Use "Doesn't match regex" with the same pattern | True SEO traffic potential |

**Audit use (seo-geo-audit):**
- Compute branded/non-branded ratio ‚Üí high branded ratio = dependency on existing awareness, weak content SEO
- Feed into E-E-A-T scoring: strong branded signal = good trust indicator

**Monitor use (seo-monitor):**
- Track monthly branded/non-branded ratio evolution
- Growth in non-branded = positive signal from SEO efforts
- Alert if branded ratio drops (reputation issue) or non-branded stagnates despite content efforts

## 2. Question Queries ‚Äî FAQ / Featured Snippets / GEO

**Purpose:** Identify featured snippet opportunities, People Also Ask targets, and AI-friendly content gaps.

### English
```
(?i)^(what|how|why|when|where|who|which|does|do|is|are|can|should|guide|tutorial|learn|explain|definition|meaning|example)
```

### French
```
(?i)^(comment|pourquoi|quand|o√π|qui|quel|quelle|quels|quelles|est-ce|combien|faut-il|peut-on|doit-on)
```

**Audit use (seo-geo-audit):**
- List question queries where the site gets impressions
- Flag those with low CTR ‚Üí content optimization target for snippet capture
- Cross-reference with FAQ schema presence on the ranking page
- Feed into GEO scoring: question queries correlate with AI citations

**Monitor use (seo-monitor):**
- Track question query volume growth (correlates with GEO / AI citation visibility)
- Alert on position drops for question queries (featured snippet loss)

## 5. Long-Tail Conversational Queries (5+ words)

**Purpose:** Detect AI query fan-out ‚Äî conversational queries generated by LLMs decomposing user questions into sub-queries.

### English
```
(?i)^(how|what|why|when|where)\s+\S+\s+\S+\s+\S+\s+\S+
```

### French
```
(?i)^(comment|pourquoi|quand|o√π|quel)\s+\S+\s+\S+\s+\S+\s+\S+
```

**Audit use (seo-geo-audit):**
- Volume of 5+ word question queries ‚Üí indicator of AI-driven traffic (query fan-out)
- Feed into GEO scoring: high long-tail volume = content is being surfaced by AI sub-queries
- Identify content gaps: long-tail queries with impressions but no clicks ‚Üí needs content optimization

**Monitor use (seo-monitor):**
- Track long-tail query growth as a proxy for AI citation expansion
- Growth in 5+ word queries post-GEO optimization = validation signal

## 6. Competitor Brand Queries

**Purpose:** Measure share of voice on competitor terms (conquest opportunities).

```
(?i)(competitor1|competitor2|competitor3)
```

> Replace with actual competitor brand names from the client profile.

**Audit use (seo-geo-audit):**
- Impressions on competitor queries ‚Üí conquest opportunity size
- CTR on competitor queries ‚Üí effectiveness of comparison/alternative content

**Monitor use (seo-monitor):**
- Track impression growth on competitor queries post-content creation

---

## Usage notes

- All patterns use `(?i)` for case-insensitive matching (RE2 syntax)
- Combine patterns with `|` (OR) to build composite filters
- GSC pattern limit is 4,096 characters ‚Äî sufficient for all patterns above
- When GSC MCP is NOT connected: these patterns cannot be applied programmatically. Instead, include them as **recommended GSC configurations** in the action plan for the client to apply manually
